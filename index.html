Welcome to the Aaroh Music Club! We are thrilled to have you here. Our club is dedicated to fostering a love for music and providing a platform for musicians of all levels to connect, collaborate, and create beautiful melodies together.

What Aaroh Club offers

  • Live Performances: Regular gigs, college fests, and cultural events where members showcase their talent.
  • Collaborative Projects: Covers, original compositions, and music videos like their rendition of Roobaroo from Rang De Basanti
  • Skill Development: Opportunities to learn instruments, vocals, sound mixing, and event management.
  • Community & Culture: A supportive environment for both seasoned performers and curious beginners.

Notable events

Previous Events

  • Music Fest 2024: A grand concert featuring performances across genres.
  • Aaroh Night: An open mic event showcasing talents of both solo artists and bands.
  • Musical Workshops: Sessions focused on music theory and practical learning

Upcoming Events

  • Annual Music Fest 2025: A major platform for live performances by club members.
  • Music Battle: Competitive event for skill-based showcasing in solo or band formats.
  • Interactive Music Workshops: Hands-on learning sessions for both beginners and advanced musicians.

Whether you're an instrumentalist, vocalist, or simply a music enthusiast, Aaroh Music Club welcomes you to join our harmonious journey. Let's create, inspire, and celebrate the magic of music together!
